Preparation of the plantain

  1. Cut both ends and cut through skin length-wise
  2. Peel. Cut into diagonal pieces. Season with a bit of salt and pepper (optional).
  3. Heat oil and when it is hot, fry the plantain pieces
  4. Toss them occasionally to ensure all are cooked
  5. When they are all brown, remove and drain in kitchen paper. Serve.
